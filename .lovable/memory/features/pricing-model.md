# FieldHands pricing model — subscription + rating-based perks

Both sides pay a subscription. Both sides are rewarded for good reviews — hirers get a discount on their plan, workers keep more of their paycheck.

## Hirer side — tiered subscription, rating discounts the price
Hirers pick a monthly plan based on how many jobs they post.

| Tier | Price/mo | Jobs/mo |
|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $29 | 1 active job |
| **Builder** | $79 | 5 active jobs |
| **Crew** | $199 | 15 active jobs |
| **Unlimited** | $399 | Unlimited jobs |

**Rating discount on the plan price** (based on rolling worker reviews of the hirer):
- 5★ → **20% off** plan price
- 4★ → **10% off** plan price
- New hirer (no rating) or 3★ and below → **full price**

Examples:
- 5★ hirer on Crew: $199 × 0.80 = **$159.20/mo**
- New hirer on Builder: **$79/mo**

No per-job booking fee. No percentage on top of the wage. The subscription IS the cost.

## Worker side — flat subscription, rating sets their take-home cut
Workers pay a locked-in **$15/month** subscription. That covers profile, vetting access, browsing, and bidding.

When a job pays out, FieldHands takes a sliding cut from the worker's payout based on their rating:

| Rating | Platform cut | Worker keeps |
|---|---|---|
| 5★ — Top Hand | 2% | 98% |
| 4★ — Verified Pro | 4% | 96% |
| **New worker (no rating)** | **5%** (default) | 95% |
| 3★ | 6% | 94% |
| 2★ | 8% | 92% |
| 1★ | 10% | 90% |

**Good work means a bigger paycheck.** That's the worker hook.

## Worked example
Job: 1 worker, 8 hrs × $25/hr = **$200 wage**
- Worker is on $15/mo plan + 5★ rating (2% cut): receives **$196** ($200 − $4)
- New worker (5% cut): receives **$190** ($200 − $10)
- Hirer pays the wage ($200) + their monthly subscription. No per-job platform fee on top.

## Copy rules
- Lead with **"subscription pricing — predictable cost, predictable income."**
- Hirers: **"Pick a plan that fits how often you hire. Earn discounts with great worker reviews."** Show all 4 tiers prominently with the 20% top-rated discount.
- Workers: **"$15/month, locked in. Your rating sets your take-home — top pros keep 98%."**
- Never say "free to join" anymore for paid sides — both sides pay subscriptions. But emphasize **free 30-day trial** if/when offered.
- Browsing jobs (workers) and posting jobs within plan limits (hirers) is included — no per-job fees beyond the subscription + worker payout cut.
- Always show workers their current take-home % in the dashboard.
- Always show hirers their current discount % (and what they need to unlock the next discount tier).
