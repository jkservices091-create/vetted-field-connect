# FieldHands pricing model

**Workers: 100% free, forever.** Free to sign up, get verified, browse jobs, apply, and get booked. Never charged anything. Never any subscription, application fee, or per-bid cost. This is non-negotiable — it's a core brand promise.

**Hirers: free to post, pay only when they book.**
- Free to sign up, create a company profile, post jobs, and review bids.
- When a hirer books a worker, FieldHands charges a **10% service fee on top of the agreed wage** (worker keeps 100% of their wage).
- Example: $25/hr × 8 hrs = $200 to worker → hirer pays $220 total ($200 to worker + $20 service fee).
- No subscriptions. No posting fees. No hidden costs.

## Copy rules
- Workers: always say "100% free" or "always free" — never "free to apply" (implies there's a paid tier).
- Hirers: "Free to post. 10% service fee only when you book." — never "Free to post. No subscriptions." alone (misleading, doesn't disclose the booking fee).
- Never frame the fee as "small" or "tiny" — just state it plainly.
- The fee covers vetting, support, and platform operations. Mention this when explaining what the fee pays for.
- Pricing page lives at `/pricing` and is linked from the public header and footer.
